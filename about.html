Meet Dr. Arash

Dr. Arash is a dedicated psychologist committed to helping individuals, couples, and families navigate emotional challenges with clarity, compassion, and practical support. His work focuses on creating a safe, respectful environment where clients feel heard, understood, and supported.

He works with people experiencing anxiety, stress, relationship difficulties, emotional overwhelm, and major life transitions. His goal is to help clients better understand themselves, strengthen resilience, and move toward healthier and more meaningful lives.

Dr. Arash’s approach combines evidence-based therapeutic methods with warmth, empathy, and careful listening. He believes that meaningful progress happens when people feel emotionally safe and genuinely supported throughout the therapeutic process.

Therapeutic Approach

A calm, collaborative, and client-centered process designed to support lasting change.
