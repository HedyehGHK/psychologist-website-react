About Dr. Arash

Dr. Arash is a dedicated psychologist focused on helping individuals and families navigate emotional challenges and improve their psychological well-being. His work emphasizes creating a calm, respectful, and supportive therapeutic environment.

With years of experience supporting clients dealing with anxiety, stress, relationship challenges, and major life transitions, Dr. Arash combines evidence-based therapy methods with compassionate listening to help people move toward healthier and more fulfilling lives.
